171 | -------- | ------------ | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
172 | 1 | Weight | <WeightMatrix> (NxN) | Any Value From 2147483647 To -2147483647 (i +- From Min/Max) |
173 | 2 | Connectivity | <ConnectivityMatrix> (NxN) | 1 If Connected, 0 If Not. |

178 > #### NodeName and VertName Cannot Be `0` (Reserved Character)
179 > #### If Vert Name Is Disabled, **DO NOT FILL IN SECTION 3**

229 ## Map Flags
230 A List Containing A Variety Of "Flags" That Can Help Describe The Limitations Of The Graph
231 > **NOTE**: All Flags Are Single Lettered And Seperated By Spaces!
232 
233 | Flag Letter | Meaning |
234 | ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
235 | F | Mandatory Flag For All Map Files (Used To Help With Data Padding) |
236 | N | Contains Negative Cycles / Negative Weights |
237 | S | Contains Self-Loops (A Node That Has A Vertex To Itself) |
238 | W | Does Not Contain Different Weight Values (All Values Same) |
239 | A | Auto Generated |
240 | X | Large (Contains More Than 64 Nodes) |
241 
242 ## Recommended Algorithm
243 The Type Of Algorithm The Map Was Designed For.
244 
245 | Key | Algorithm Type |
246 | ---- | ------------------------ |
247 | SP | Shortest Path |
248 | MST | Minimum Spanning Tree |
249 | APSP | All Pairs Shortest Path |
250 | FLOW | Minimum Flow |
